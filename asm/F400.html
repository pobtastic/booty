F400: Interrupt Low-Order Byte Jump Table
Used as the low-order byte when an interrupt is generated. The high-order byte is also FE meaning all interrupts jump to AliasInterruptRedirect.
See GameEntryPoint, Handler_Interrupts and AliasInterruptRedirect.
